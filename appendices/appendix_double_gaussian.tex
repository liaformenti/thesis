\section{Gaussian of double gaussian fit}
% --------------------------------------------------

The distribution of residuals should be modelled by a double gaussian fit:
$$ G(r) = A_{s}exp\left[ \frac{-(r-\mu)^{2}}{2\sigma_s^{2}} \right] $$
